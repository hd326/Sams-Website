Help & FAQS

GENERAL

+ How does Active Clean 1 work?

We Clean. You Relax


Active Clean 1 is a cleaning company that has provided an easy way to book, schedule, and manage all your cleaning service needs in one place. Our cleaners are insured and bonded, and tained to deliver on Active Clean 1's 52 point checklist.


Ready to relax? You can book here.Once you have booked with our services, we will send one of our cleaning professionals to make your place clean as possible.

+ Do the cleaning professionals undergo background checks?

Without a question. We only hire cleaners based on the highest level of professionalism, clean abstract that are reliable. We understand that it makes a lot to trust someone with your house and we do absolutely all that we can to put your mind at ease. We run a strict background check on every member during the interview and vetting process.

+ Does someone need to be present?

Whether you are home or not during the time of cleaning is up to you! When you finish a booking with us, you'll be prompted to give us entry instructions. If you have a doorman or can hide a key somewhere, there's no need for you to be home. Or, you may feel free to stay around during the cleaning. Whatever you're comfortable with is fine with us. We're here to serve you.


In any case, please don't forget about your appointments. We'll remind you with an email or text message, but if the cleaner can't get in the door by following your entry instructions, or if you aren't home when you said you would be, you'll be charged a lock-out fee.

+ Is Active Clean 1 insured and bonded?

Yes, Active Clean 1 is insured and bonded. We understand that it is a privilege to be in your home, and we are always careful. In the unlikely event that an object is damaged, please notify us within 48 hours of when the appointment is completed either by email (r_duong89@yahoo.com) or by phone (916) 704-4061

+ Is Active Clean 1 Pet Safe

Here at Active Clean 1, we love animals, but they wont always love us. If you think your pet may become overly anxious while we are there, please make temporary arrangements while we are in your home. You can leave detailed pet instructions during the online scheduling process to let us know how to proceed.

CLEANING

+ Do I need to provide supplies for your service?

We use the best supplies and almost all equipment needed for your cleaning needs, with only a few exceptions.

We do not provide with us:

  • Vacuum;
  • Conventional mop and bucket
  • Stepping stool
  • Toilet brushes; or
  • Specialty products, including, but not limited to:
    • Heavy-duty bathroom cleaner;
    • Wood cleaner
    • Bleach; or
    • Stainless steel cleaner.


We are happy to use these items, if provided by you, and left out in clear view for our cleaning staff and accompanied by direcionts on their use.

+ What if I don't have the proper tools for cleaning

Our cleaners bring a travel wet/dry mop that will be used on non-carpeted areas. If you do not have a vacuum, the cleaner will not be able to properly clean any carpeted areas.

+ What if I just had renovation work done on my home?

We provide a customized post-construction clean for these types of situations. Please contact us at (916) 704-4061, or email customer service at r_duong89@yahoo.com to coordinate this and receive pricing information.

+ Can I give specific instruction to the cleaners and ask for custom requests?

Yes, you can leave special instructions for our cleaning professional when you schedule your appointment online. After the clean is complete, you can rate your experience with us and provide feedback to us at r_duong89@yahoo.com

THE #1 CLEAN GUARANTEE

+ What is the #1 Clean Guarantee?

If you don't feel satisfied with our cleaning, we will come out and re-clean(within a 72 hour window). If you still don't think we did a good enough job to recommend us to your friends, we'll refund your money.

+ How can I request a reclean?

Customers can request a reclean by emailing r_duong89@yahoo.com or by phone (916) 704-4061, as long as the appointment was completed within the past 48 hours. The reclean appointment must be scheduled and completed within 72 hours of contact.

Please let us know where we fell short. Our customer service team will respond promptly.

+ How many recleans can be booked using the #1 Clean Guarantee?

There is no specific limit on how many recleans a customer can receive. We want to ensure fair use of the benefit and guarantee a great experience every time. With that said, we reserve the right to require additional time and budget for future cleans and/or void the guarantee for future cleans (if requested excessively)

+ What is and is not eligible for #1 Clean Guarantee?

Appointments that are completed within the past 48 hours are eligible for the Sparkly Guarantee.

Not Eligible are:

  • Appointments completed more than 48 hours ago.
  • Items not included on 50 point check list
  • Free cleans
  • Recleans
  • Homes not in standard condition (e.g. biohazards, hoarding, etc.)
  • Extra services not booked for original appointment
  • Post-construction/post-renovation cleans
  • Move-out cleans

BILLING AND PAYMENT

+ How do I pay for my cleaning?

We accept all major credit and debit cards with a MasterCard, Visa, Discover Network, American Express logo.

We do not accept cash payments or checks. Tips may be given in cash form (optional).

+ What is your cancellation policy?

We assess a $50 cancellation fee to appointments not cancelled by 5PM the day before the appointment, unless that clean is a specialty clean. For all specialty cleans (including but not limited to post-construction/post-renovation cleans), a cancellation fee amounting to 50% of the price quoted for that specialty clean will be applied to appointments not cancelled by 5PM the day before the appointment.

+ When does my card get charged?

Your credit card will be charged on the day after the appointment takes place. You will receive an automated email the with transaction summary to notify you when and how much your card is charged.

+ How do I update my billing information?

To change your billing information, simply, log into our online portal using your username and password and edit your billing information in the "Settings" section of the website.

+ Is my billing information kept safe and secure?

Our website has an SSL certificate which is required to process electronic payments. Credit card transactions are processed by Stripe and in also layered in on their own 256-bit security protocol. In addition, no credit card numbers are stored in our system (only a token that allows us to charge the card). Rest assured that we take security with dire seriousness.
