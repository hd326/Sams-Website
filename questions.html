Help & FAQS

GENERAL

+ How does Active Clean 1 work?

We Clean. You Relax


Active Clean 1 is a cleaning company that has provided an easy way to book, schedule, and manage all your cleaning service needs in one place. Our cleaners are insured and bonded, and tained to deliver on Active Clean 1's 52 point checklist.


Ready to relax? You can book here.Once you have booked with our services, we will send one of our cleaning professionals to make your place clean as possible.

+ Do the cleaning professionals undergo background checks?

Without a question. We only hire cleaners based on the highest level of professionalism, clean abstract that are reliable. We understand that it makes a lot to trust someone with your house and we do absolutely all that we can to put your mind at ease. We run a strict background check on every member during the interview and vetting process.

+ Does someone need to be present?

Whether you are home or not during the time of cleaning is up to you! When you finish a booking with us, you'll be prompted to give us entry instructions. If you have a doorman or can hide a key somewhere, there's no need for you to be home. Or, you may feel free to stay around during the cleaning. Whatever you're comfortable with is fine with us. We're here to serve you.


In any case, please don't forget about your appointments. We'll remind you with an email or text message, but if the cleaner can't get in the door by following your entry instructions, or if you aren't home when you said you would be, you'll be charged a lock-out fee.

+ Is Active Clean 1 insured and bonded?

Yes, Active Clean 1 is insured and bonded. We understand that it is a privilege to be in your home, and we are always careful. In the unlikely event that an object is damaged, please notify us within 48 hours of when the appointment is completed either by email (r_duong89@yahoo.com) or by phone (916) 704-4061

+ Is Active Clean 1 Pet Safe

Here at Active Clean 1, we love animals, but they wont always love us. If you think your pet may become overly anxious while we are there, please make temporary arrangements while we are in your home. You can leave detailed pet instructions during the online scheduling process to let us know how to proceed.
